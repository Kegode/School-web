About Us

Our School

Golden Tots is about people.Catering for children between the ages of 3 and 15 years, Golden Tots offer a friendly learning environment steered by well qualified teachers to provide a rich blend of academic and extracurricular activities.

Our goal is to offer children sound primary education, while teaching them about their rich Kenyan culture through carefully chosen class activities. Play is an integral part of our curriculum, and we ensure well structured indoor, and out-door play. The outdoor facilities allow for children to swing and slide, and enjoy sand and water play, while the indoor facilities as well as equipment provide for a blend of make believe play opportunities.

History

The school was started in the year 2005 by The Bramwels.It was formed with the target of bringing out the best society in the area

Since then the school has been able to grow wings under their leadership. As at now more than 1000 people have passed through the school. Many teachers have acquired skills as they were being mentored by Directors.

Departments

There are several indoor and outdoor departments within the institution

The school has a boarding section with dormitories for both girls and boys. The section has a motherly matron who oversees the pupils needs in the area.

English department is the core of the institution. There are competent teachers in the department who make sure that the queens language is passed with flying colors

Kiswahili department has always been the best performing department in the school since 2013. The department has produced great kiswahili speakers in the country

This is the department which has always been the most improving since 2015. The department boosts of great teachers

The department comprises of proven teachers in the area. Mathematics being the pillar in any problem solving has been given the serious concentration it needs
